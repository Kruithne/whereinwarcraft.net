We use the information we collect to:

- Display submitted scores

- Comply with legal obligations

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties, except:

We retain your information for as long as necessary to display game scores and as required by applicable law.

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

You can control cookie settings through your browser, but some features may not work properly if you disable cookies.
